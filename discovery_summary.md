# Discovery Summary 
After our team interviewed 17+ people, we came to the conclusion that it is in our best interest to focus on News and the Local Davis Experience. A lot of people expressed interest in local news and what events are going on in Davis rather than relying on friends and third-party sources to get information that may or may not be true. We realized that a lot of people do not read local newspapers like "The Aggie" because it is a lengthy read and most of the time it takes too long to get unimportant information. Therefore, if we focused our app on creating a way for quick news of the day, it might encourage students to read local news and also engage with events if they see their friends are going. As for the games, there was some remote interest but it seems more of a niche rather than an essential for our app. We will still implement a local leaderboard, but after that we won't expand too much until we get our core components down for the release.
