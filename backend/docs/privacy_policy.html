TapIn - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

TapIn ("we", "our", "the app") is a campus life app built for UC Davis students. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your name, email address (via Apple or Google Sign-In), or phone number (via SMS authentication). This is used solely to create and manage your account.

Profile Information: Your display name, which appears on game leaderboards and within the app.

Gameplay Data: Game scores, completion times, and streaks for leaderboard functionality.

Saved Content: Articles and events you bookmark are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively for app functionality:

  • Authenticating your account
  • Displaying your name on leaderboards
  • Storing your game scores and streaks
  • Enabling personalized features like saved content

We do not use your data for advertising, tracking, or any purpose beyond the app's core features.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple Sign-In — for authentication
  • Google Sign-In — for authentication
  • Google Cloud Firestore — for storing leaderboard data and user accounts

We do not share your data with advertisers or analytics providers.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Cloud infrastructure. Saved articles and events are stored locally on your device. We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

Children's Privacy

TapIn is intended for college students. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
